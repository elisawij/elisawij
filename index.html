Elisa Wij

Elisa Wij (she/her) is a graphic designer based in Brussels. Her practice includes web design & coding, digital content, editorial design and other printed matter – feel free to make suggestions! She likes to bridge the gap between clean and playful with a concept-first approach, often focusing on typography. Elisa is available for commissions from individuals, artists, organisations, (small) businesses... mostly in the cultural sector. Don't hesitate to get in touch!
